Ice Hockey

Hockey is a very fascinating topic. Professional hockey is called NHL which stands for National Hockey League. Hockey uses sticks and pucks. Sticks are a very well designed way to shoot the pucks. Pucks are even frozen before each game so they can slide on the ice easier.

NHL is played in both Canada and United States of America. People from every country can play in the NHL if they have the skill to play ice hockey.
